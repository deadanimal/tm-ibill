Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

What is SSM BizTrust?
BizTrust is a standard that can be obtained by online business entities via SSM, which certifies that the entity has complied with the characteristics of the Trust Principles and Criteria that have been established including registration with SSM, online security and privacy of information.
Who can apply to SSM BizTrust?

Any online entity that is registered and in good standing with SSM and follows established business practices.

What online medium should the online business entity have?

Any online entity that offers products or services or even to simply share information on their website, including online transactions and social media.

How do I access the SSM BizTrust system?

SSM BizTrust can be accessed via www.ssm.com.my and click the icon for “SSM BizTrust” under e-Services list or directly to https://biztrust.ssm.com.my

What are the software and hardware is required for using SSM BizTrust Services?

(a)The Hardware Requirements are as below:

  • Intel iCore 3 & above processor
  • 2 GB of memory and above
  • 1Mbps minimum internet connection
  • Printer (Black or Color)
  • Scanner

(b)The Software Requirements are as below:

  • Microsoft Windows 7 or above
  • Web Browser
  • Chrome version 32 or higher
  • Firefox version 27 or higher
  • Internet Explorer version 8, 9 or 11
  • Adobe Reader 9.0 and above
